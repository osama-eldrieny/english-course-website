Section 6
CTA Strategy — No Direct Payment

All calls-to-action lead to WhatsApp or a Waiting List form. No payment gateway needed in Phase 1.

📱 WhatsApp CTA (Primary)

Each course page has a "Enroll via WhatsApp" button. Clicking opens a pre-filled WhatsApp message that includes the course name, so the instructor knows what the student is interested in.

wa.me/+60XXXXXXXXX?text=Hi!+I'm+interested+in+the+Intensive+English+Course

⏳ Waiting List (Secondary)

For courses that are not yet open or full (e.g., group courses waiting for minimum 5). Collects name, email, phone. Simple form per course page.

Google Form or Typeform embedded · Auto-notify when seats open
Important note to agency: Do NOT implement any payment gateway, checkout, or booking system in Phase 1. All financial transactions happen off-site (via WhatsApp conversation). This keeps the site simple and fast to launch.
Section 7
Referral Program

🎁 Refer a Friend — Earn a Reward

The referral program encourages word-of-mouth. Details (discount % or reward type) to be defined by the client, but the website should include a dedicated section or page.

1
Student gets a unique referral link or code after enrolling
2
They share it with friends who sign up via WhatsApp or waiting list
3
When friend enrolls, referrer earns a discount or session credit

Phase 1 can use a simple manual referral (student mentions a name in their WhatsApp message). A formal referral tracking system can be added in Phase 2.
